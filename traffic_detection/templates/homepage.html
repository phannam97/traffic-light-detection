Self-driving cars are a promising and relatively feasible idea in the near future. This vehicle is an application of AI that has a number of challenges such as recognizing traffic lights, signs, unclear road markings, pedestrians, etc. These problems can be overcome by using technological developments in the areas of Deep Learning. In this paper, we propose a model based on deep neural networks to detect and identify reliable traffic lights by using learning transfer. Trained model of data set containing different images of traffic signals according to Traffic Signal of Korea. The model accomplishes its goal by detecting traffic lights. The identification process is divided into four stages and we propose an extension method for each stage. The stages are: ROI, color detection and signal classification.
